Who is SHINee?

SHINee made its debut as a South Korean Kpop group on 25 May 2008. SHINee consists of five members: Onew, Johnghyun, Key, Minho, and Taemin. Johnghyun passed away on 18 December 2017. The other four members have continued producing and recording content with their latest single, Poet | Artist, released on 25 May 2025 in celebration of the 17th anniversary of their debut.

What is Kpop?

Kpop is short for Korean Pop music. Kpop began in the 1990s and has since spread to become a global music market. Typically, Kpop groups consist of girls or boys, and within each group there are individual roles. These roles are usually (but not limited to) leader, main vocal, main dancer, rapper, and the "face" of the group.
